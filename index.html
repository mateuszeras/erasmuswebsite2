What is Erasmus+?

Erasmus+ is a European Union (EU) program that has been supporting education, training, youth, and sport across Europe since 2014. It offers opportunities for students to study or do internships abroad, while teachers, trainers, and other staff can take part in teaching and training activities. Young people can also join international exchanges, volunteering projects, and cooperation initiatives focused on youth. In addition, Erasmus+ promotes the development of sport by supporting projects that encourage physical activity and address challenges such as inclusion, diversity, and equal opportunities. What our school offers: group mobility / student exchange (with our partner schools in Spain, Norway, and Finland), student mobility from 1 to 12 months, teacher training.
